# Complete Initiative

Mark the current initiative as completed and archive it.

## Process
1. Verify all tasks are completed
2. Update `.brains/active.json` to mark initiative as completed
3. Clear the active initiative (so a new one can be started)
4. Generate a summary of the completed work

## Output
- Updated initiative status
- Completion summary
