# Clarify Specification

Your task is to identify underspecified areas in the current specification and ask clarifying questions.

## Guidelines
1. Review the specification for ambiguous requirements
2. Identify missing edge cases or scenarios
3. Ask up to 5 highly targeted questions
4. Focus on questions that will impact implementation
5. Encode answers back into the specification

## Output
- List of clarifying questions
- Update spec.md with clarified requirements
